Victoria park

Victoria park located west of Leicester city is a quite large park with 3 main fields on these fields we can find goal post for rugby and football, a cricket green and a few play parks for children.

There are many good reasons to come here as there is New walk over the road which is a great way to get to city centre, it is great for students as the university of Leicester is connected to the park.

There are still bits of history at Victoria park like the arch of remembrance which is a first world war memorial near the entrance, and there are information boards around the park which will show more of this history.

I would say this park is the best for children and younger men to play sports or on the park, there is also a tennis court, football court and a skate park.
