New walk

New walk is long stretch that goes straight into the city centre on this walk we can find lovely Victorian houses; you can also see all the Victorian lamppost that are all up it.

It is nice and quite so good place to get away from the busy city centre it also leads up to Victoria park so is a nice versatile walk as you can get to city centre and to Victoria park and is a lot better than walking up London road where there is traffic and is very noisy.

This walk is great for taking kids and the elderly as there are many benches and a few little fields on the way to stop and rest, also you can take your dog down here if you are taking it to Victoria park because it is spacious and has places for your dog to go toilet.

To summarize New walk is a nice long path to get to and from city centre where you can take your dog and children to stay away from the main roads and you can view the history of Leicester.
