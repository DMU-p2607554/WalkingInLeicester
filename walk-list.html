River Soar

This walk has such a versatile scenary with veiws of insustrial leicster and along the rive soar, where you can reach the grand union canal that leads to abbey park which is an amazing park to have a picknick and walk around.

Abbey park

Abbey park is a very nice location to walk and enjoy as it is a large park with many things to see and do, perfect place for summer as you can set up a picnic or just enjoy the sun.

Victoria park

This is a nice place to walk close to the city centre with an outdoor gym and other sport facilities which can be used by the public. Lots of fun play parks for children as well there is much to do on this park.

New walk

New walk is a nice walk to the city centre recommended scenic route to city centre it is quiet, and you can see some of the history of Leicester with its old architecture and Victorian lamppost.
